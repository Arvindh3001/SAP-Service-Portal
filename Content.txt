SAP SERVICE MANAGEMENT PORTAL - COMPLETE APPLICATION PROMPT
BUSINESS CONTEXT
Create a comprehensive service management portal for an Authorized Service Provider (ASP) managing field technicians who service HP printers and IT equipment for enterprise customers across India. The system handles the complete lifecycle from technician onboarding to case resolution.

USER PERSONAS
1. Field Technician (Ravi Kumar)
Role: On-ground service engineer
Needs: Register in system, complete training, view assigned cases, update service status
Access Level: Limited to own cases and profile
2. ASP Admin/Manager (Amit Sharma - "AS")
Role: Service center administrator
Needs: Oversee all operations, manage technicians, assign cases, approve requests, monitor performance
Access Level: Full visibility across all cases, technicians, and operations
APPLICATION STRUCTURE & FLOW
The application has THREE MAIN FLOWS:

FLOW A: TECHNICIAN ONBOARDING (New Technician Journey)
FLOW B: TECHNICIAN OPERATIONS (Daily Work)
FLOW C: ADMIN OPERATIONS (Management & Oversight)
