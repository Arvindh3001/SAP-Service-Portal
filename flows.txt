================================================================================
  SAP SERVICE PORTAL — PROTOTYPE FLOWS
  Technician Flow  |  ASP (Admin) Flow
================================================================================


════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  FLOW 1 — TECHNICIAN
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  PART A · Onboarding (New Technician Registration)
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Step 1 · Login
    Screen  : Login
    Route   : login (default)
    Action  : Click "Register as Technician"  →  Step 2

  Step 2 · AI Voice Onboarding
    Screen  : AI Voice Onboarding
    Route   : aiVoiceOnboarding
    Purpose : Conversational AI ("Ava") walks the technician through the
              registration steps. Language selector, live voice-to-form fill,
              mic toggle (green = idle, red = listening).
    Action  : Click "Next: KYC Documents"  →  Step 3

  Step 3 · Personal Details
    Screen  : Onboarding — Personal Details
    Route   : onboardingPersonal
    Purpose : Collect name, DOB, gender, email, mobile, address, experience,
              and zone preference.
    Action  : Click "Next: KYC Documents"  →  Step 4

  Step 4 · KYC Documents
    Screen  : Technician KYC
    Route   : technicianKyc
    Purpose : Upload Aadhaar, PAN, certification, passport photo, and address
              proof. Each document shows upload status (Uploaded / Verified /
              Pending).
    Action  : Click "Next: Skills & Zone"  →  Step 5

  Step 5 · Skills & Zone
    Screen  : Skills & Zone
    Route   : skillsZone
    Purpose : Select skill chips from preset categories (HVAC & Mechanical,
              Electrical & Power, IT & Networking) or add custom skills via
              text input. Set service zone, secondary zone, experience level,
              specialisation, certification level, and availability.
    Action  : Click "Next: Review & Submit"  →  Step 6

  Step 6 · Review & Submit
    Screen  : Onboarding Review
    Route   : onboardingReview
    Purpose : 3-column summary of Personal Details / KYC Documents / Skills &
              Zone, each with an Edit button. Warning strip if any document is
              pending. Consent checkbox.
    Action  : Click "Submit Application"  →  Step 7

  Step 7 · Learning Journeys
    Screen  : Learning Journeys
    Route   : learningJourneys
    Purpose : 6 course cards (SAP Learning portal style). Mandatory, Partner,
              and Free badges. Live Session / Learning Journey / Course types.
              Courses cover HVAC, SAP Service Cloud, Electrical, Customer
              Service, Safety & Compliance, and AI Field Service.
              Complete all courses before going live.
    Action  : Click "Continue to Dashboard"  →  Step 8


  PART B · Daily Work (Active Technician)
  ────────────────────────────────────────

  Step 8 · Technician Home
    Screen  : Technician Home
    Route   : technicianHome
    Purpose : Welcome banner, Quick Actions (View Cases / Accept Case /
              Mark En Route / Update Status / Request Parts / My Learning),
              My Cases by Stage (Assigned / En Route / On Site / Parts
              Pending), Today's Performance KPIs (resolved tickets, fix rate,
              SLA risk, customer rating + SLA compliance bar), and Today's
              Scheduled Jobs table.
    Actions :
      → Click "View Cases" or any job row  →  Step 9
      → Click "My Learning"               →  Step 7 (Learning Journeys)
      → Click "AI Voice Assistant"        ��  Step 11

  Step 9 · Technician Case Details
    Screen  : Technician Case Details
    Route   : technicianCase
    Purpose : Full case info — customer, product, issue, priority badge,
              status flow bar, technician notes timeline, parts used, and
              customer satisfaction rating.
    Actions :
      → Click "Edit Case"         →  Step 10
      → Click "AI Assistant" mic  →  Step 11
      → Click back / Exit         →  Step 8

  Step 10 · Technician Case Edit
    Screen  : Technician Case Edit
    Route   : technicianCaseEdit
    Purpose : Edit service notes, add/remove parts, update labor time, change
              case status, upload photos/evidence. Save & Complete closes the
              case.
    Actions :
      → Click "Save Changes"      →  Step 9
      → Click "Save & Complete"   →  Step 8 (case closed)
      → Click "Cancel"            →  Step 9

  Step 11 · AI Voice Service Assistant
    Screen  : AI Voice Assistant
    Route   : aiVoiceAssistant
    Purpose : Hands-free assistant for on-site use. Speech-to-text for case
              notes, error code lookup, wiring diagrams, parts ordering, and
              step-by-step repair guidance.
    Action  : Click Exit  →  Step 8


  Back Navigation
  ───────────────
  Each screen has a nav-back button in the top bar that follows the
  reverse of the flow above.


════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  FLOW 2 — ASP (ADMIN / AUTHORIZED SERVICE PROVIDER)
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  Step 1 · Login
    Screen  : Login
    Route   : login (default)
    Action  : Click "Sign In as ASP"  →  Step 2

  Step 2 · ASP Dashboard (Main)
    Screen  : Main Dashboard
    Route   : main
    Purpose : Command center. KPI tiles (Active Cases, Technicians Available,
              SLA Compliance, Avg Resolution Time, Customer Satisfaction,
              Revenue). Quick Action tiles (Technicians, Approvals, Cases,
              Reports, Inventory, Allocate). Full case table with all
              technicians' cases. Alerts panel (critical, warnings, updates).
              Team leaderboard.
    Actions :
      → Click "Technicians" tile      →  Step 3
      → Click "Approvals" tile        →  Step 4
      → Click "Allocate" tile         →  Step 5
      → Click any case row "View"     →  Step 6
      → Click any case row "Assign"   →  Step 5

  Step 3 · Technician Management
    Screen  : Technician Management
    Route   : technicianMgmt
    Purpose : IconTabBar with 3 tabs:
              • Active Technicians — table with Name, Emp ID, Skills, Zone,
                Status (colored badge), Active Cases, Attendance (colored
                badge), SLA%, and Edit/View actions.
              • Onboarding Pipeline — technicians in training.
              • Offboarded — historical records.
              Top bar has "Add New Technician" button.
    Actions :
      → Click "Add New Technician"    →  Flow 1, Step 2 (AI Voice Onboarding)
      → Click "Edit"                  →  Edit technician (toast: coming soon)
      → Click "View"                  →  View profile (toast: coming soon)
      → Click nav-back                →  Step 2

  Step 4 · Admin Approvals
    Screen  : Admin Approvals
    Route   : adminApprovals
    Purpose : Tabbed approval queue — Leave Requests, Expense Claims, Parts
              Orders, New Technician Applications. Each item shows detail,
              urgency, and Approve / Reject / Request Info actions.
              Summary stats: total pending, urgent today, avg approval time.
    Action  : Click nav-back  →  Step 2

  Step 5 · AI Auto Assignment
    Screen  : AI Auto Assignment
    Route   : aiAutoAssignment
    Purpose : AI recommendation engine for case-to-technician matching.
              Alert banner shows the unassigned urgent case. Left panel shows
              case details. Center panel shows ranked technician matches with
              match score, reasoning across 15 factors (skills, ETA, workload,
              customer history, language, etc.), and predicted outcome. Right
              panel shows bar-chart score breakdown.
    Actions :
      → Click "Assign to [Technician]"     →  Step 2 (with toast confirmation)
      → Click "Override & Manual Assign"   →  Manual assignment
      → Click nav-back                     →  Step 2

  Step 6 · Admin Case Details
    Screen  : Admin Case Details
    Route   : caseDetails
    Purpose : Admin view of a case — same case info as technician view PLUS
              assignment history (AI match score, alternatives considered),
              business metrics (financial details, SLA tracking, customer
              impact), admin action panel (Reassign, Escalate, Extend SLA,
              Contact Customer, Generate Report, Audit Log), and full customer
              account details (contract, SLA terms, service history).
    Actions :
      → Click "Edit Case"           →  Edit mode
      → Click "Assign Technician"   →  Step 5
      → Click nav-back / Exit       →  Step 2


  Back Navigation
  ───────────────
  Every screen has a nav-back button in the top blue bar that returns to
  the Main Dashboard (Step 2).
